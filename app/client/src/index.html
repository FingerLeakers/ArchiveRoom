View your own archive in 3 easy steps with Google BigQuery!

Step 1

Go to Google APIs, login.
Click "Create Project", if asked.
Under "Services", turn on BigQuery API:

Step 2

Go to githubarchive:github.timeline table and click "Query Table" in the top right corner. Paste this query, change "USERNAME" to your Github username: 

SELECT type, created_at FROM [githubarchive:github.timeline]
WHERE actor = "USERNAME"

Hit "Run Query" and "Download as CSV".

Step 3

Input your GitHub username, upload the CSV file and click "View Archive"
